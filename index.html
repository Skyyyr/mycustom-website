Become a Web Developer

As a Code Platoon student, you will be challenged and rigorously trained in our immersive full-stack software engineering Bootcamps. Professional software engineers teach each class. The curriculum features lectures, pair programming, and self-study. When you have finished our curriculum, you will have a solid foundation to launch a software engineering career. We focus on holistic training, with part of our curriculum covering soft skills and personal development. From programming languages to industry best practices, you will get hands-on experience working with today’s web development tools.
